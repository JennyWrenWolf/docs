Parsing from Body or Payload

In most cases, you will be pulling the content of the message from the body or payload. For REST type messages, this will be available in POST or PUT methods. For other protocols, you may only receive the payload. Once you have the body or payload, you need to determine the format of the content. Is it Text in one format or another or is it Binary?
