RabbitMQ

Another option for listening for device messages is our Hosted RabbitMQ listener. We have created a plugin module for RabbitMQ for tight integration with NuvIoT.

Have your own RabbitMQ server? We also offer a listener that you can use to subscribe to messages from a third party installation
